Asian Pasta

Description

Cool pasta salad on a hot summer day is the perfect BBQ side dish. It is always one of the first things to go.

Ingredients

  • soy sauce
  • carrot
  • brown sugar
  • oyster sauce
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add in garlic, onions and saute till golden.
  2. Add in cabbage and carrots and mix well.
  3. Add in salt, sugar, soy sauce and mix well.
  4. Add in pasta and toss well.
  5. Add in vinegar and stir fry for 2 to 3 mins.
